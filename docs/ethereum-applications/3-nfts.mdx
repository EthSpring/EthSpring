### Quests

Here are a list of applications you can try out (this does not mean we endorse any of the project). For a more comprehensive list, see "[Quick overview of the NFT Ecosystem](https://andrewsteinwold.substack.com/p/-quick-overview-of-the-nft-ecosystem)".

- [ ]  [CryptoKitty](https://www.cryptokitties.co/) - buy and breed a CryptoKitty.
- [ ]  [Nifty.ink](https://medium.com/@austin_48503/nifty-ink-%EF%B8%8F-alpha-release-c860a4904cb2) - Austin's project, a scaffold-eth build for creating an NFT platform that scales. Draw something on [nifty.ink](http://nifty.ink) and mint the NFT, send it to another address; upgrade it to the Ethereum mainnet, and sell it on [OpenSea](https://opensea.io/); use your ENS name to sign your artwork for true authenticity.
- [ ]  [OpenSea](https://opensea.io/) - NFT marketplace. Purchase an NFT on OpenSea.
- [ ]  [ENS](https://ens.domains/) - Ethereum name service ([ENS as NFT](https://docs.ens.domains/dapp-developer-guide/ens-as-nft)). Reserve an ENS name and link it to your Ethereum address.
- [ ]  [Zora](https://zora.co/) - an open protocol to curate and exchange NFTs. See "[how to build your own auction house](https://zora.mirror.xyz/UwQwplCMEe1T5eUkp0CpTDJjZXvAK3eeakskTaQe3pE)".
- [ ]  [Foundation.app](https://foundation.app/) - a platform that allows creators to sell limited-edition goods, such as clothing, art, music, and really anything at all.
- [ ]  [Mirror](https://mirror.xyz/) - a publishing platform to [crowdfund writing with NFTs](https://dev.mirror.xyz/llJ_AboiDJwB_ZAFz-CXU7fL2p8v5Wz6MjfluLafewQ). Built on top of Zora.
- [ ]  [Async Art](https://async.art/) - digital art marketplace for programmable art and [music](https://help.async.art/en/articles/5177648-async-music).
- [ ]  [Hashmasks](https://www.thehashmasks.com/) - a living digital art collectible.
- [ ]  [Niftex](https://www.niftex.org/)- [Fractional ownership](https://blog.niftex.com/nft-fractions-a-primer/) of NFTs.
- [ ]  [Aavegotchi](https://aavegotchi.com/) - combines DeFi and NFT gaming where each Aavegotchi character represents a user's collateral that is dposited within the lending platform Aave.
- [ ]  [CryptoPunks](https://www.larvalabs.com/cryptopunks) - 10,000 uniquely generated characters launched in mid-2017.
- [ ]  [Bord Ape Yacht Club NFT](https://www.cnet.com/how-to/the-bored-ape-yacht-club-nft-collection-everything-you-need-to-know/) - act as both avatars and tickets to an online social club.
